Snowman

Instructions for playing Snowman

Snowman is a simple word guessing game. Players try to figure out an unknown word by guessing letters. If seven letters which do not appear in the word are guessed, the snowman falls apart (and loses). Try and keep your snowman alive by guessing the correct letters. HINT: All of the words relate to the themes of Christmas or winter. Good luck!
